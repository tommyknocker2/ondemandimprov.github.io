Meet the Band

The Rounder Circle came together in the spring of 2024 to perform at the Stanford farm. Since then, the band has been playing together regularly, honing their sound and writing lots of new music. Their music blends traditional bluegrass with modern influences, creating a unique sound that resonates with audiences of all ages. In June 2025, The Rounder Circle spent a day in the studio recording their debut album. Keep your eyes out for it on streaming platforms later this year!
